We define **word** as a set of characters other than newline('\n', 13) and space(' ', 32), with a maximum length of 50 characters.
We define **sentence** as a set of word, seperated by space ' ', with zero or more commas ',', and ends with a dot '.', with a maximum length of 500 characters, including commas, dots, and spaces.
We define **paragraph** as a set of sentences of 6, without new lines.
